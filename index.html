About Us

Sweet Chills F&B started on the 3rd of August, 2020. Founded by two women DIVYA JAIN and RIDHI AGARWAL who were very keen on revisiting the old school days with the ice sip-ups, summer afternoons and the fun times with friends.The regular flavors were everywhere but the traditional flavors which have health benefits too were lost in the past.Thus, SNOSICLE®️ was born with a twist to the classics. It took 4 months on Research and Development of the flavors, packaging and branding before we finalized the fantastic flavours! And finally, on the 6th of December, 2020, we launched the first SNOSICLE®️ boxes in retail market.
